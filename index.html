How to Use

  1. Click the buttons or type numbers directly from your keyboard.
  2. Use CE to clear the last entry, C to reset the entire calculation.
  3. Special keys: 1/x (reciprocal), x² (square), √x (square root), % (percentage).

Worked Examples

  • Percentage discount: 200 − 10% → 180
  • Tip calculation: 45 × 15% → 6.75
  • Square root: √(144) → 12

FAQ

Is this calculator accurate?

Yes, it uses standard JavaScript math operations. However, for high-precision scientific or financial needs, specialized tools may be required.

Can I use it offline?

Yes, if you save this page to your device—it runs entirely in your browser.

What does the % button do?

It calculates the percentage of the number to its left in the expression, similar to most standard calculators.
